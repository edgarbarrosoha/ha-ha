# Legacy: ha-knowledge-graph

## Vision
Make the invisible structure of knowledge visible. Every HA vault has a topology — clusters, bridges, islands, highways — that its creator can't see by reading files one at a time. The knowledge graph reveals it.

## Long-term Impact
- **For HA:** The diagnostic tool. "Your Community dimension is isolated from Technology" is a prescribable insight.
- **For TaaS:** Graph topology becomes an API. Any client can query the structure of any HA instance.
- **For research:** Network analysis on HA graphs yields publishable insights about knowledge organization.

## Lineage
- Inspired by Obsidian's native graph view but reimagined through HA axioms
- Theoretical foundation: pentagram.md (dimensions as staff lines)
- Technical heritage: vis.js for rendering, Python for parsing
