# Legacy: HA Score

Before Guido d'Arezzo, music was oral — ephemeral, local, unteachable at scale. His pentagram changed everything: five lines, infinite compositions. HA Score is that moment for complex endeavors.

## The Aspiration

A living orchestral score where every action across every HA vault becomes a note on the staff. Six dimensions are six orchestral sections. Time flows left to right. Cross-dimensional activity on the same date creates barlines — the system's heartbeat made visible. Narrative movements give shape to what would otherwise be noise.

The endgame: an AI agent that doesn't just visualize the past but *composes* the future — proposing what the next bars should contain, animating them into existence, turning strategic planning into musical composition.

## The Gap

Dashboards show numbers. Gantt charts show tasks. Neither captures the *simultaneity* of complex work — the fact that on February 15, Legacy, Technology, and Community were all active at once, reinforcing each other. No tool shows you the polyphony of your own life. HA Score does.

## The Unique Angle

Only a composer would think to render a life as an orchestral score. Only an HA creator would have the six-dimensional staff to write it on. Only someone who built a federated AI framework would imagine an agent that composes the next movement. This is Edgar's entire trajectory — music, systems thinking, AI — converging in a single artifact.
