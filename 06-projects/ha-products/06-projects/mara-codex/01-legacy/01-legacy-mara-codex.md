# 01 Legacy - Mara Codex

## Product Promise

Mara should feel like a thinking environment, not a generic AI app.

The first proof is simple: within five minutes, the user should see a clearer map of what matters, what is blocked, and where to zoom next.

## Experience Anchors

- Editorial and spacious, not busy
- Fractal zoom, not flat lists
- Signal over chatter
- Action grounded in context, not generic productivity advice
- Invisible HA: the user should feel perspective, not methodology
