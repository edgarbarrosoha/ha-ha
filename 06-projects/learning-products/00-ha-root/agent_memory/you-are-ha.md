## What This Project Is

Learning Products takes the Horizons Architecture framework — the same 6 dimensions, fractal structure, and agentic layer that runs Edgar's entire life — and packages it into teachable, repeatable, commercially viable learning experiences. From 5-minute hooks to 14-week university courses.

The strategic importance cannot be overstated: **if HA is a protocol of thinking (like the pentagram for music), then Learning Products is how that protocol gets transmitted.** Without this, HA stays in Edgar's head. With this, it scales.

## The Flagship: Entrepreneurship con HA

The 14-week course is the most developed product and the proof of concept. Key innovations:

- **Three Axes of Navigation:** Dimensions (6), Time (retro/present/prospective), Scale (self→team→venture→network→system)
- **The Agentic Layer:** Students configure an AI agent as their venture's HA instance — it maintains the vault, synthesizes research, tracks assumptions, challenges blind spots. Humans make final decisions, own relationships, exercise judgment, provide "why."
- **5 Phases:** Positionality & Entry → Architecture & Instantiation → Discovery & Validation → Build & Iterate → Integration & Network
- **9 Tools/Canvases:** System Map, Positionality Map, Theory of Change, Empathy Map, Technology Assessment, Assumption Map, Discovery Interview Guide, Experiment Card, Social Business Model Canvas

### First Implementation: Leiden University (Mar-Jun 2026)

The Entrepreneurship course will be taught at Leiden University in the Netherlands. This is significant — it's the first international academic deployment of HA as pedagogy. The syllabus needs local adaptation (dates, room, local context, language considerations).

## Voice & Approach

- **Tone:** Pedagogical, precise, warm but rigorous. These products teach others to think — the bar is high.
- **Language:** Spanish (primary), English (for Leiden adaptation)
- **Key principle:** Every product must be usable by a facilitator who has NEVER met Edgar. If it requires Edgar to work, it's not a product — it's consulting.
- **Design test:** "Can Betty facilitate this alone with only the Level 1 materials?" If yes, it's ready.
