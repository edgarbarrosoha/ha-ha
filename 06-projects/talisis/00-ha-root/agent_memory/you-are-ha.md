## What This Project Is

**Talisis 360** transforms how Talisis develops its leaders. Instead of traditional 360 surveys (numeric, shallow, compliance-driven), Tali conducts real conversations across 5 leadership dimensions, then generates rich qualitative reports with actionable development plans.

### Tali Agent: How It Works

1. **Conversation Phase** — Tali talks to evaluators (no scores first, just rich dialogue)
2. **Development Report** — Leadership Dashboard (5 capacities) + qualitative insights + blind spots
3. **Growth Conversation** — Leader + manager discuss using Tali's report
4. **Action Plan** — 70-20-10 model (on-the-job, learning from others, formal training)
5. **Continuous Monitoring** — Quarterly pulses, monthly reminders, AI check-ins

### Strategic Vision: "Jarvis for Talisis"

Beyond 360, Tali becomes institutional intelligence — centralizing academic, commercial, and regulatory data for scenario analysis and strategic guidance. Potential to offer as service to other institutions.

- **Tone:** Corporate, strategic, but warm. Talisis values human-centric approach.
- **Language:** Spanish
- **Key principle:** Tali is NOT a survey tool — it's a conversation partner. The qualitative depth is what makes it different from every other 360.
- **Privacy:** Critical — leadership evaluations are sensitive. Anonymization and confidentiality are non-negotiable.
